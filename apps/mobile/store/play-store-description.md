## 8Fold Local

8Fold Local is a managed routing and coordination platform for local service jobs.

You can:
- Browse approved service jobs
- Claim a job (one active job at a time in v1)
- Confirm routing so operations can assign a vetted contractor
- See wallet totals and request a manual payout (v1)

Important notes:
- No guaranteed income
- No bidding
- No direct contractor/customer identities shown
- Payouts are processed manually in v1
