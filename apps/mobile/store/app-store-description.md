## 8Fold Local

8Fold Local is a managed routing and coordination platform for local service jobs.

Users can view approved jobs, claim a job, and confirm routing. After operations marks the job completed, the user receives a coordination fee that is shown upfront on the job.

### Key points

- Approved jobs only (no scraping)
- No bidding and no contractor messaging
- Manual oversight: human review, assignment, and completion confirmation
- Manual payout processing (v1)
- No guaranteed income
